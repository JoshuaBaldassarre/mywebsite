About

The aim of mywebsite is to give anyone who is learning HTML and CSS, or someone who has some experience with it, a chance to practice and learn through a pre-made website that includes all the basic functionality of a website in a light, simple and pretty package. It is also made to be used as a template for any sort of website, and is intended to be highly personalizable with little effort and even knowledge on CSS and HTML.*

This website was made completely voluntarily in my free time, donating here to support and motivate further development for this project would be greatly appreciated.
